Performance

The BMW X5M Competition is powered by a high-performance 4.4-liter V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology, delivering an impressive 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque.

This powertrain allows the X5M Competition to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs in its class.

Equipped with an eight-speed M Steptronic transmission and the M xDrive all-wheel-drive system, the X5M Competition offers exceptional agility and handling, whether on the track or winding roads.

The Adaptive M Suspension and Active M Differential further enhance performance by optimizing traction, stability, and cornering dynamics.

Safety

The BMW X5M Competition prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).

Standard safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and pedestrian detection.

Additionally, the X5M Competition offers optional safety enhancements such as BMW's Driving Assistant Professional package, which includes features like Traffic Jam Assist, Lane Keep Assist, and Evasion Aid.

The SUV also comes equipped with a 360-degree camera system and parking assistance to aid in maneuvering and parking in tight spaces.
