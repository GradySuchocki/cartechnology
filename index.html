Performance

The BMW X5M Competition is powered by a high-performance 4.4-liter V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology, delivering an impressive 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque.

This powertrain allows the X5M Competition to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs in its class.

Equipped with an eight-speed M Steptronic transmission and the M xDrive all-wheel-drive system, the X5M Competition offers exceptional agility and handling, whether on the track or winding roads.

The Adaptive M Suspension and Active M Differential further enhance performance by optimizing traction, stability, and cornering dynamics.

Safety

The BMW X5M Competition prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).

Standard safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and pedestrian detection.

Additionally, the X5M Competition offers optional safety enhancements such as BMW's Driving Assistant Professional package, which includes features like Traffic Jam Assist, Lane Keep Assist, and Evasion Aid.

The SUV also comes equipped with a 360-degree camera system and parking assistance to aid in maneuvering and parking in tight spaces.

Comfort

The BMW X5M Competition offers a luxurious and comfortable interior designed to provide an unparalleled driving experience.

With premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship, the cabin exudes sophistication and refinement.

Standard comfort features include heated and ventilated front seats, a panoramic sunroof, four-zone automatic climate control, and ambient lighting.

For added convenience, the SUV offers power-adjustable seats, a hands-free power liftgate, and a wireless charging pad for compatible smartphones.

Infotainment

The BMW X5M Competition features the latest in-car technology to keep you connected, entertained, and informed on the road.

At the heart of the infotainment system is a large touchscreen display with BMW's iDrive interface, providing intuitive access to navigation, audio, and vehicle settings.

The SUV also offers smartphone integration with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto, allowing you to seamlessly access your favorite apps and music.

Other notable features include a premium sound system, wireless charging, and a heads-up display that projects important information onto the windshield.

Design

The BMW X5M Competition boasts a bold and athletic design that commands attention on the road.

From its aggressive front grille and sculpted lines to its aerodynamic body kit and quad exhaust pipes, every aspect of the SUV exudes sportiness and performance.

The interior design is equally striking, with a driver-focused cockpit and luxurious appointments throughout.

Available exterior paint colors and wheel options allow you to customize the X5M Competition to suit your personal style.

Technology

The BMW X5M Competition is packed with advanced technology features to enhance safety, convenience, and performance.

Key technology highlights include BMW's Driving Assistant Professional suite, which offers features like adaptive cruise control, lane-keeping assist, and traffic jam assistance.

The SUV also features BMW's Live Cockpit Professional system, which combines a digital instrument cluster with a high-resolution touchscreen display for navigation, media, and vehicle settings.

Other notable technology features include a wireless charging pad, gesture control, and a customizable ambient lighting system.

Specifications

Here are the key specifications for the BMW X5M Competition:

  • Engine: 4.4-liter V8 with M TwinPower Turbo
  • Horsepower: 617 hp
  • Torque: 553 lb-ft
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 3.7 seconds
  • Transmission: Eight-speed M Steptronic
  • Drive Type: M xDrive all-wheel drive
  • Suspension: Adaptive M
