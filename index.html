Performance

The BMW X5M Competition is powered by a high-performance 4.4-liter V8 engine with M TwinPower Turbo technology, delivering an impressive 617 horsepower and 553 lb-ft of torque.

This powertrain allows the X5M Competition to accelerate from 0 to 60 mph in just 3.7 seconds, making it one of the fastest SUVs in its class.

Equipped with an eight-speed M Steptronic transmission and the M xDrive all-wheel-drive system, the X5M Competition offers exceptional agility and handling, whether on the track or winding roads.

The Adaptive M Suspension and Active M Differential further enhance performance by optimizing traction, stability, and cornering dynamics.

Safety

The BMW X5M Competition prioritizes safety with a comprehensive suite of advanced driver-assistance systems (ADAS).

Standard safety features include adaptive cruise control, lane departure warning, forward collision warning, automatic emergency braking, and pedestrian detection.

Additionally, the X5M Competition offers optional safety enhancements such as BMW's Driving Assistant Professional package, which includes features like Traffic Jam Assist, Lane Keep Assist, and Evasion Aid.

The SUV also comes equipped with a 360-degree camera system and parking assistance to aid in maneuvering and parking in tight spaces.

Comfort

Inside the cabin, the BMW X5M Competition offers a luxurious and comfortable environment for both driver and passengers.

Featuring premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship, the interior boasts plush leather upholstery, heated and ventilated front seats, and a panoramic glass roof that floods the cabin with natural light.

With ample legroom and headroom in both the front and rear seats, along with customizable ambient lighting, the X5M Competition ensures a relaxing and enjoyable ride, even on long journeys.

The SUV also features quad-zone automatic climate control, allowing passengers in each seating area to set their preferred temperature for optimal comfort.

Infotainment

The BMW X5M Competition comes equipped with the latest infotainment technology to keep you connected and entertained on the road.

At the heart of the system is a large touchscreen display with BMW's intuitive iDrive interface, providing easy access to navigation, multimedia, and vehicle settings.

Wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto compatibility come standard, allowing seamless integration with your smartphone for hands-free calls, messaging, and music streaming.

For audiophiles, the X5M Competition offers an optional Harman Kardon surround sound system or a premium Bowers & Wilkins Diamond surround sound system, delivering concert-quality sound throughout the cabin.

Design

The BMW X5M Competition features a bold and aggressive design that reflects its high-performance capabilities.

From its muscular proportions and sculpted lines to its distinctive M-specific elements, such as the enlarged kidney grille, aerodynamic body kit, and quad exhaust pipes, the X5M Competition exudes sportiness and sophistication.

The SUV rides on large M-specific alloy wheels, further enhancing its stance and presence on the road.

Inside, the cabin combines luxury and functionality, with premium materials, ergonomic controls, and advanced driver-focused features.

Technology

The BMW X5M Competition is packed with advanced technology features to enhance performance, safety, and convenience.

Key technology highlights include:

  • BMW Live Cockpit Professional with a fully digital instrument cluster and a high-resolution touchscreen display
  • Head-Up Display projecting vital driving information onto the windshield
  • Gesture Control for intuitive operation of various functions with simple hand gestures
  • BMW ConnectedDrive services for remote access to vehicle functions and services via smartphone app
  • Advanced driver-assistance systems such as Traffic Jam Assist, Lane Keep Assist, and Active Cruise Control with Stop & Go

Specifications

Here are the key specifications of the BMW X5M Competition:

  • Engine: 4.4-liter V8 with M TwinPower Turbo technology
  • Horsepower: 617 hp
  • Torque: 553 lb-ft
  • Acceleration (0-60 mph): 3.7 seconds
  • Transmission: 8-speed M Steptronic automatic
  • Drive Type: M xDrive all-wheel drive
  • Length: 195.2 inches
  • Width: 78.9 inches
  • Height: 68.9 inches
